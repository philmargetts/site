I got better at piano by not playing for a year

[link—standalone]

I sat down at the piano again the other day, making it the first time in quite a while. Expecting something pretty rough, I was surprised to notice a more sensitve and considered touch than I remembered (forunately, someone else was listening in and could confirm this feeling).

I pondered on this for a little while, and noted that I'd been playing significantly more guitar in the last 12 months. Guitar being my main instrument, I summised that focussing my musical efforts in one direction allowed me to mature musically without worrying - to as great a degree - about how to "format" the musical syntax for the instrument. Musicians who have dabbled in instruments outside of their primary focus will understand this, I think. This musical maturation achieved on the guitar then translated across to the piano.

Now what is this syntax? I suggest you watch Adam's video :)

Towards a personal language of music - Adam Neely

I also played the drums for the first time in six months a few weeks before, and I think a similar observation held true then, as well.

Now am I a master pianist or drummer (or guitarist, for that matter)? Definitely not, but I found this observation interesting nonetheless.

A new blog (again)

[link—standalone]

It is with some nostalgia that I write another introductory blog post... Hosted back on GitHub pages, no less!

As mentioned on the home page, what I didn't like about my implementation of WordPress was that it put me in a box. And considering a future where I am likely to diversify outside of educational technology, I wanted a personal site that could be a reflection of me more holistically. Having a site dedicated to "work things" is great, but as the home page says, I'd like a place where I also can jot down thoughts and be able to look back on how they (I) have evolved. I must admit there is also some attraction to having a web presence outside of mainstream social media, where there is more control - and I can have a basic HTML site if I want to!

Feel free to reach out!
