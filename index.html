About

My name is Phil and I’m currently employed as an IT Pro in education and finishing a Bachelor's degree in IT and Business.

I have a passion to see technology help people in their day-to-day. In the education space, learning is the goal. Technology should be there to support the learning, not direct it.

An analogy I enjoy sharing is that technology is just a tool that can be used for good or not so good. Just think: a hammer can be used to build a house or bludgeon someone to death!
