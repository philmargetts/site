Why is this site 'ugly'?

Fundamentally, function over form (while being very mindful of the application!)

In my option, having a functional, readable site puts much more emphasis on the content, rather than the presentation. I guess this reflects my belief that fundamentally, technology is a tool. Does [insert generally-disregarded solution] perform the task required? Great!

Am I suggesting this is best practice for every site? Definitely not, but we'll see how it goes for this one.
